TradeGroupSizeAlertsPlugin

TradeGroupSizeAlertsPlugin

Monitors trade group events on the terminal's symbol and emits alert events when groups are seen with size greater than the configured threshold. The threshold is made available to other modules as a alerts.group-trade-size setting via SettingsPlugin
